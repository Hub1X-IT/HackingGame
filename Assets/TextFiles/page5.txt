Hexadecimal Encoding Method

Average popularity, similar to decimal.
Uses ASCII Table, converts symbols to
hexadecimal numbers.
You may see symbols from 0-9 and A-F
where A = 10, B = 11, C = 12, D = 13,
E = 14, F = 15.

Ranges to remember:
A-Z ~ 41-5A (Includes A-F)
a-z ~ 61-7A (Includes A-F)
0-9 ~ 30-39 (Does not include A-F)

One symbol encoded in hexadecimal
has two numbers or one number and
one letter.
