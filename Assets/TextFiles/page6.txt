Octal Encoding Method

Average popularity, similar to decimal.
Uses ASCII Table, converts symbols to
octal numbers.
You may see symbols from 0-8.

Ranges to remember:
A-Z ~ 101-132
a-z ~ 141-172
0-9 ~ 060-071

You might have already noticed that
one symbol is a three-digit number.
Remember that there is no "9" in
octal encoding method!
